I´m Nati Ciraolo, born in the spring of the 90s. I have been studying dance since I was a child, at the age of 14 I started my career as a Musical Comedy Performer Musical Comedy and the Dance Interpreter career at the Caleidoscopio Institute. From that moment on I constantly trained in dance with various teachers in Argentina, such as Julieta Rodriguez Grumberg, Soledad Rodriguez Grumberg, Soledad Galoto, Emmanuel Ludueña, among others. Specializing from an early age in Contemporary Dance and improvisation. When I finished high school I started a career in Choreographic Composition at the U.N.A. at the same time I was training as a Pilates instructor at the Pilatesol Institute. In parallel to my university career and my work as a teacher, I participated in various artistic projects, presenting in 2015" Distorsión" my first dance theater play as director and coreographer premiered at La Lunares theater. From 2016 I started my Yoga training in Ohakune, New Zealand and culminated with the TTC200HS in Rishikesh, India in 2019. I trained as a Shiatsu masseuse to complement my knowledge about the body in Buenos Aires at the "Shiatsu por el Mundo" school. And in 2017 I became certified as a massagist in Thailand at the ancient school "Wat Pho". As a dancer I perfected my skills in Berlin and Toulouse with international teachers such as Christina Martzani, Marion Sparber and Alan Fuentes Guerra, among others. I currently work as a teacher, dancer and choreographer.
